Background...

Daniel Hernandez is an attorney of Mexican descent who resides in San Diego, CA. He is the youngest son of six with his family originating from Jalisco, Mexico. He identifies as a gay cisgender man as well as a LAMB(Mariah Carey fan) and has a partner of 8 years. He enjoys multiple hobbies such as vinyl record collecting, going to festivals, learning Mandarin Chinese, traveling, hiking, reading books, watching the Real Housewives series, photography, and listening to “god’s gift to humanity,” Mariah Carey.

He studied at Santa Ana College, Boston University, UCI, and California Western School of Law. While traveling abroad he visited many countries such as the UK, France, Portugal, Mongolia, Spain, Italy, Austria, the Czech Republic, Croatia, Japan, Korea, Vietnam, India, Tibet, Nepal, Thailand, and China where he spent 2 years teaching English. His favorite place to travel to was Tibet where he had the opportunity to spend three days in a buddhist temple. While there he communed with the monks and made a golden mandala which currently hangs proudly in his apartment. Today, he works as a Staff Attorney for Disability Rights California. He enjoys his work advocating for those who don’t have a voice or the underserved communities. His greatest accomplishment is graduating law school, passing the California BAR, traveling the world, and climbing Mt. Fuji from the base to the peak. His dream is to win the lottery, run a non-profit, and own a home along the northern California coastline.
